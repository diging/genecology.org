Contribute

The Genecology Project is a collaborative, open-note research project, and we're eager for your contributions! You can reach Erick at erick.peirson@asu.edu.

If you are a scholar with a perspective to share on the people, places, research, or contexts that intersect the story of ecological genetics in Britain and beyond, please don't hesitate to write.

We are especially excited to hear from people who have conducted research in the field of ecological genetics, regardless of the stage of career. Oral histories and written recollections are an important source of data for this project. We're eager to talk to you!

Finally, we're always on the lookout for archival material lurking in dark corners. If you have come across material that seems relevant, please let us know. If you have material to share (in digital or physical form), we would be very grateful to receive it.

Ecologist Patricia J. Watson
(Scottish Plant Breeding Station)
